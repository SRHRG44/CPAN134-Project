Erza

Erza Scarlet, a key character in Fairy Tail, serves as one of the two tritagonists alongside Gray Fullbuster. As an S-class Mage of the Fairy Tail Guild, her loyalty to Makarov and the guild stems from her challenging past, including being enslaved in the Tower of Heaven with her childhood friend Jellal Fernandes. Erza, voiced by Sayaka ÅŒhara in Japanese and Colleen Clinkenbeard in English, is described as tall and beautiful, with scarlet hair and dark brown eyes. Her Requip Magic allows her to change outfits and armors at will, showcasing her strength and versatility in battle. Despite her strict demeanor, Erza is warm-hearted and fiercely protective of her friends, particularly Gray and Lucy. Her past traumas shape her fears and insecurities, but she ultimately finds strength in her determination to safeguard those she cares about. In battle, Erza's skills in Sword Magic, Requip Magic, and Telekinesis make her a formidable opponent. Despite facing challenges, her endurance and strategic intellect remain unwavering. Throughout her journey, Erza's character evolves, displaying resilience, compassion, and an unwavering commitment to her friends and guild, earning her respect and admiration.

Gray

Gray Fullbuster is a Mage from the Fairy Tail Guild and a member of Team Natsu. With spiky black hair and dark blue eyes, Gray's toned physique is a defining trait. Notably, he bears scars on his forehead and abdomen, along with a dark blue guild stamp below his collarbone. In terms of attire, Gray is known for his variability, often seen wearing a white coat. However, he consistently sports a necklace resembling a sword, a metal bracelet, and a chain attached to his pants. Personality-wise, Gray is laid-back yet capable of seriousness when necessary. He shares a friendly rivalry with Natsu, but their bond runs deep, reflecting genuine concern for each other. While loyal to Fairy Tail, Gray's tendency to trash talk is notable, along with his peculiar habit of unconsciously removing his clothes at inappropriate times. In terms of abilities, Gray specializes in Ice-Make Magic, allowing him to create and shape ice into various forms, both offensive and defensive. With mastery over Ice-Make, Gray can produce a multitude of objects and manipulate foreign ice for his advantage. Wendy

Wendy Marvell, a young female Sky Dragon Slayer, was chosen as a Dragon Slayer four hundred years prior to X777. She served as her foster mother's vessel to travel to X777 through the Eclipse Gate as part of a plan to exterminate Acnologia. Initially part of the Cait Shelter Guild, Wendy officially joined Fairy Tail after the events surrounding Nirvana. Following Fairy Tail's disbandment, she and Carla joined Lamia Scale, but Wendy later left Lamia Scale to help resurrect Fairy Tail. Physically, Wendy is petite with fair skin, long dark blue hair, and brown eyes. She usually wears a simple dress with wing-like attachments around her arms and legs. During her time in Edolas, she sports pigtails held by animal ear-like attachments and wears a long-sleeved top with a frilled skirt and thigh-high socks. Wendy is characterized as shy, polite, and eager to make friends. She deeply cares for her guild members and shows a growth in confidence and bravery over time. Intelligent and honest, Wendy is easily swayed by others and has a romantic side. Unlike other Dragon Slayers, she dislikes fighting but will do so for the sake of her guild and friends. Wendy suffers from motion sickness, preventing her from casting healing spells on herself during travel. Throughout her journey, Wendy evolves from a timid girl to a more confident and determined individual, willing to fight alongside her friends and face challenges head-on.
